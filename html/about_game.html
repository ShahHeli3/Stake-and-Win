How to play ?

  1. Install MetaMask
  2. Connect your MetaMask wallet
  3. Pay the entry fee to join the game
  4. Select a number on which you want to stake your Eth
  5. Wait for the results...

Winner

  • The game generates a winning number.
  • If your number matches with the winning number, the winning amount will be transferred to your registered MetaMask wallet.
  • If there are multiple winners, the winning amount will be distributed amongst them.

Winning amount

  • The winning amount depends on the total participants of that round.
  • 20% of the total amount will be transferred to the owner and the rest will be divided amongst the winner/s.
